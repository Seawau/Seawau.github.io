My Goals

After I graduate, I plan to get a masters in psychology that focuses on clinical psychology, and a doctorate if it’s required. Ideally, I want to work as a therapist for children and young adults. Mental and emotional struggles impact everyone throughout points of our life–big or small. Depression, among other mental health challenges, can occur just as easily, and the effect they have had on those I know has spurred me on to understand these psychological issues. As I pursue this goal throughout my schooling, I hope to expand my academic exposure through courses and research opportunities to determine my specializations, interests, and insight. I am eager to take on new experiences in the psychology field, be it research or hands-on training.
