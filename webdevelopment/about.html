About Me

My Experience With Psychology

Outside of school, I tend to be the “psychologist” of my friend group. I spend quite a bit of time in introspection, so perhaps they think I’m insightful, or maybe I’m just a good listener. The more I work through mental and emotional processes with people, the more enlightened I feel. Yet, I also feel I know nothing at all. Although I’m not an expert in therapy, I believe I’m on the right track. Doing research is also essential to applying practice in the field. During my schooling, I have developed researching and writing skills, analyzed statistics using SPSS, and condensed scholarly articles. Reading the experiences of others with mental health struggles has also clued me into perspectives of the world that I would not have gained on my own. In my free time writing creatively, it has also taken a fair amount of time to understand the psychology of intentions and reactions of characters, as well as the inner workings of the world and society.
